Our Story

Born to Brew.
Made for Lahore.

Double Shot was born in 2018 with a simple obsession — to bring world-class specialty coffee to Lahore's streets. From a single location in Cantt, we've grown into the city's most-loved coffee destination, driven by bold flavours and an uncompromising commitment to craft.

Every bean is freshly roasted. Every drink is handcrafted. Every bite is made with care. Whether you're an early riser chasing your first shot, or a night owl craving something warm — Double Shot is your place.
